## Buy With Confidence, Not Regret

A used car can be a great value—or an expensive mistake. Our Pre-Purchase Inspection gives you the information you need to make a smart decision. We've saved countless customers from buying problem vehicles, and helped others negotiate better prices.

## Our 150+ Point Inspection

### Exterior Assessment

- **Body Panel Alignment** – Signs of accident repair
- **Paint Condition** – Checking for repaints and blending
- **Glass Inspection** – Chips, cracks, and seal condition
- **Lighting Systems** – All bulbs and lenses checked
- **Rust Inspection** – Surface and structural corrosion

### Undercarriage Examination

- **Frame Inspection** – Looking for damage, rust, and repairs
- **Suspension Components** – Wear and damage assessment
- **Exhaust System** – Leaks, rust, and hanger condition
- **Fuel & Brake Lines** – Corrosion and damage check
- **Fluid Leaks** – Oil, transmission, coolant, power steering

### Engine & Drivetrain

- **Engine Compression** – Cylinder health (when warranted)
- **Fluid Condition** – Oil, coolant, transmission, brake
- **Belt & Hose Inspection** – Age and wear assessment
- **Cooling System Test** – Pressure test and thermostat function
- **Engine Noise Analysis** – Listening for concerning sounds

### Interior & Electronics

- **All Controls & Switches** – Every button tested
- **HVAC System** – Heat, AC, and all fan speeds
- **Power Accessories** – Windows, locks, seats, mirrors
- **Entertainment System** – Radio, speakers, connectivity
- **Warning Lights** – Verified all illuminate then extinguish

### Computer Diagnostics

- **Full System Scan** – All modules checked for codes
- **Pending Codes** – Issues waiting to trigger a light
- **Readiness Monitors** – Were codes recently cleared?
- **Freeze Frame Data** – History of past problems

### Road Test

- **Acceleration** – Smooth power delivery
- **Braking** – Stopping distance and pedal feel
- **Steering** – Alignment and response
- **Transmission** – Shift quality and timing
- **Suspension** – Handling and ride quality
- **Unusual Noises** – Identified and diagnosed

## What We Look For

| Red Flag               | What It Means            |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Mismatched paint       | Possible accident repair |
| Recently cleared codes | Seller hiding problems   |
| Milky oil              | Head gasket failure      |
| Transmission slip      | Expensive repair ahead   |
| Frame damage           | Safety and value concern |
| Flood damage signs     | Walk away immediately    |

## Inspection Red Flags

We recommend walking away if we find:

- Frame damage from accidents
- Signs of flood damage
- Odometer tampering evidence
- Major engine or transmission issues
- Extensive rust in structural areas
