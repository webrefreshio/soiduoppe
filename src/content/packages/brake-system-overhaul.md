## Warning Signs You Need This Service

Pay attention to these symptoms:

| Symptom                     | Possible Cause                        |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Squealing or grinding noise | Worn brake pads                       |
| Soft or spongy pedal        | Air in brake lines or worn components |
| Vehicle pulls to one side   | Uneven brake wear or caliper issue    |
| Vibration when braking      | Warped rotors                         |
| Brake warning light on      | Low fluid or sensor triggered         |
