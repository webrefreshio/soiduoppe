## Summer Driving Tips

**Managing Heat:**

- Park in shade when possible
- Use a windshield sun shade
- Crack windows slightly when parked
- Start AC on fresh air, then switch to recirculate

**Long Distance Driving:**

- Check tire pressure when cold (morning)
- Monitor temperature gauge in traffic
- Listen for unusual sounds
- Stop immediately if warning lights appear
