## More Than Just a Car Wash

Our full detailing service restores your vehicle to showroom condition, inside and out. Whether you're preparing to sell, attending a special event, or simply treating yourself, we'll make your car look brand new.

## Exterior Detailing

### Hand Wash & Dry

- Two-bucket wash method to prevent scratches
- pH-balanced soap safe for all finishes
- Microfiber drying to prevent water spots

### Paint Correction

- Clay bar treatment to remove contaminants
- Machine polish to remove light scratches
- Swirl mark removal for a mirror finish

### Protection

- Premium carnauba wax or ceramic sealant
- UV protection to prevent fading
- Hydrophobic coating for easy cleaning

### Wheels & Tires

- Brake dust removal from wheels
- Tire cleaning and dressing
- Wheel well detailing

## Interior Detailing

### Deep Cleaning

- Complete vacuuming including crevices
- Steam cleaning of carpets and mats
- Upholstery shampooing (fabric) or conditioning (leather)

### Surface Care

- Dashboard and console cleaning
- UV protectant application
- Vent and button detail cleaning

### Glass & Trim

- Streak-free interior glass cleaning
- Trim restoration and protection
- Door jamb cleaning
