## Never Get Stranded Again

Your car battery powers everything from starting your engine to running your electronics. A failing battery can leave you stranded at the worst possible moment.

## Warning Signs of a Dying Battery

- **Slow engine crank** – The engine turns over sluggishly
- **Clicking sound** – You hear clicks but the engine won't start
- **Dim lights** – Headlights and interior lights are dimmer than usual
- **Dashboard warning** – Battery or check engine light illuminated
- **Swollen battery case** – Heat has damaged the battery housing
- **Old age** – Most batteries last 3–5 years

## Our Battery Service Process

1. **Battery load test** – We measure cranking amps and reserve capacity
2. **Charging system check** – Testing the alternator output
3. **Terminal inspection** – Checking for corrosion and loose connections
4. **Cable condition** – Ensuring proper power delivery
5. **Professional installation** – Correct fitment and secure mounting
6. **Safe disposal** – Eco-friendly recycling of your old battery

## Extend Your Battery Life

- Avoid short trips that don't fully recharge the battery
- Turn off lights and electronics when the engine is off
- Keep terminals clean and free of corrosion
- Have your charging system checked annually
