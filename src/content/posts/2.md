## Winter Driving Challenges

Winter weather presents unique challenges for vehicle owners. From freezing temperatures to snow and ice, your car needs special preparation to handle these conditions safely and reliably.

## Battery Health Check

Cold weather is hard on batteries. A battery that tests fine in summer may fail in winter. Our comprehensive battery testing includes:

- Load testing to measure cold cranking amps
- Terminal cleaning and corrosion removal
- Charging system verification
- Replacement recommendations

## Fluid and Lubrication

Winter-grade fluids prevent freezing and ensure proper lubrication:

- Antifreeze concentration testing
- Oil viscosity check for cold starts
- Transmission fluid condition
- Power steering and brake fluid inspection

## Tire Safety

Winter tires provide crucial traction on snow and ice:

- Tread depth measurement
- Tire pressure adjustment for cold weather
- Rotation for even wear
- Spare tire inspection

## Heating and Defrosting

A properly functioning heating system is essential:

- Heater core and blower motor check
- Defroster verification
- Window seal inspection
- Cabin air filter replacement

## Emergency Preparedness

Winter emergencies require preparation:

- Ice scraper and snow brush
- Emergency kit with blankets and water
- Jumper cables and flashlight
- First aid supplies
