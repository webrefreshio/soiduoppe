## The Cost of Skipping Inspection

Buying a used car without inspection is like gambling with thousands of dollars. Hidden problems can cost more than the vehicle itself to repair.

## Our 150-Point Inspection

### Exterior Assessment

- Paint condition and blending
- Body panel alignment
- Glass and lighting systems
- Rust and corrosion inspection

### Undercarriage Examination

- Frame and structural integrity
- Suspension components
- Fuel and brake lines
- Exhaust system condition

### Engine and Drivetrain

- Compression testing
- Fluid analysis
- Belt and hose inspection
- Cooling system pressure test

### Interior and Electronics

- All controls and switches
- HVAC system functionality
- Entertainment and safety systems
- Warning light verification

### Road Test Evaluation

- Acceleration and braking
- Steering and handling
- Transmission operation
- Unusual noises or vibrations

## Computer Diagnostics

### System Scanning

All vehicle computers checked for codes and data.

### Freeze Frame Data

Historical problem information.

### Readiness Monitors

Verification that systems are functioning properly.

## What We Look For

### Accident Damage

- Frame straightening
- Panel replacement
- Paint overspray

### Flood Damage

- Water stains and corrosion
- Electrical system issues
- Carpet and upholstery damage

### Odometer Fraud

- Inconsistent wear patterns
- Service record verification
- Title history check

## The Inspection Report

### Detailed Findings

Photographs and descriptions of all issues.

### Cost Estimates

Repair pricing for identified problems.

### Safety Assessment

Immediate safety concerns highlighted.

### Buy/Don't Buy Recommendation

Honest assessment based on findings.

## Negotiation Leverage

Use inspection results to:

- Justify lower price offers
- Request seller repairs
- Walk away from problematic vehicles
- Make informed purchase decisions

## Timing Your Inspection

### Before Purchase

Never buy without inspection.

### After Offer Acceptance

But before finalizing the deal.

### Independent Inspection

Always use your own trusted mechanic.

## The Value of Professional Inspection

A $149 inspection can save you thousands in repair costs and prevent buying a lemon. Knowledge is power when buying a used vehicle.
